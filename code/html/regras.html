Community Rules

Please read and follow these guidelines to keep PFPedia safe for everyone

1

No NSFW Images

All content must be safe for work. No explicit, suggestive, or adult content of any kind.

2

Fictional Characters Only

Only upload profile pictures of fictional characters from games, anime, manga, cartoons, or other creative works.

3

Profile Pictures Only

Only upload photos intended to be used as profile pictures. No banners, memes, screenshots, or unrelated images.

4

File Requirements

Images must meet the file requirements:

  • Maximum file size: 5MB
  • Dimensions: 256×256 to 2048×2048 pixels
  • Formats: JPEG, PNG, or WebP
5

Moderation Process

All pictures are analyzed and approved by a moderator before being public on the website.

6

No Harmful Content

No content promoting hate, harassment, extreme violence, illegal activities, or self-harm.

7

Rights & Permissions

By uploading, you confirm you have the right to use the image and grant us permission to display, review, and remove it according to our policy. We may strip EXIF metadata to protect user privacy.
