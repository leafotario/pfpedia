FAQ

PFPedia is an independent profile picture archive of fictional characters where you can easily find profile pictures of your favorite characters.

All profile pictures are submitted by users and come from publicly available materials, official media, or promotional content.

PFPedia itself doesn't create or own any of the images — we just host what the community uploads.

PFPedia does not own any characters, trademarks, screenshots, or artwork shown on the site. All rights belong to their original creators, studios, and copyright holders.

We only host content for informational and non-commercial purposes.

If you're a copyright holder and want something removed, contact us and we'll take care of it.

For copyright removals, reports, or just general help, please contact us through e-mail: support@pfpedia.online

All images on PFPedia go through a thorough analysis by our moderation team before being approved, so you'll rarely find something inappropriate here.

If you do find something wrong, contact us at support@pfpedia.online with evidence and we'll look into it.

To know what you can or can't upload on PFPedia, please check our Rules page by clicking on the "Rules" button at the top of the site.

PFPedia doesn't collect any personal data besides the absolute basics needed to create and manage your account.

We don't track you, sell info, or gather anything unnecessary. Apart from what's required for login and user features, nothing else is stored.
